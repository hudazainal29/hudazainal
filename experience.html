Source:  TGV Cinema The Mines

 

  		TGV

I am working in TGV The Mines when I was waiting for my SPM result for 6 months. It's near from my house and only take 10 minutes to arrive there. To be honest TGV is my last choices. I don't like to work in Cinema because it's crowded and noisy. Plus, I don't like to watch any movies. For me, it's better to watch it at home. However, I still accept this job to gain working experience before enter university rather than waste my time at home doing nothing. Let me share with you what I have learned in TGV Cinema.

In TGV, there are 5 department which is:  
  1. Counter (Candy Bar)
  2. Ticketing
  3. Kiosk
  4. Hall
  5. Pop corn Maker
And I always be responsible to handle ticketing department. There is a lot of things that I need to remember. For example, the promotion. Most of people that come to ticketing counter will pay using credit or debit card. I am the one who responsible to know what kind of promotion that have been available during that time. Its hard because the promotion have the limit time and expired date. I always need to inform to the customer the important date and always remind them to renew their membership.

Basically, customer who use Maybank card can get 15% discount from the real amount of ticket or food (Every combo). However, it only can be pay using Maybank Debit/Credit card. This promotion only available on Monday until Thursday, 8am - 6pm Other card also have the benefits but not same as Maybank. Usually, customer will use CitiBank and most of them is Chinese customers. For me, Maybank card can give many benefits compared to other card. Therefore, if you like to watch movies in Cinema don't forget to bring it. If not, then create Maybank account now.

Last, I need to renew user membership, the customers only need to pay RM 10 for renew. However, to create a new membership it can be done by technical manager, not general manager or staff. If customers card is missing then we don't take any responsibilities of it. We only can advice them to create a new one haha!

Ohh yes, forget to mention that I am work as part timer and I am only working for 4 hours. However, if there are too many customers on that day then I will be working untill late night. Depend on what kind of movies is available on that day.



TESCO

If asked whether to choose TGV or TESCO I will choose TESCO!. Sadly, I only work there for 2 months (part-time) during my semester break

Same as TGV, Tesco also have 5 department which consist of:  
  1. Ambient
  2. Cashier
  3. Fresh
  4. System
  5. Bakery
I am the only girl that working in Ambient Department. The task is difficult for girls to do it because we need to carry a big and heavy things. Every staff in Ambient department need to be prepared, to get things from the store and arrange it. To be honest, it's boring because we need to wait and facing things. It also depends on total of customer that come into TESCO.

Why I said its fun than working at TGV? cause TESCO hired many staff girls even we are not in the same department. I still can make a new friends with someone in the same age or 2 years older than me. They're mature, understanding and helpful. The general manager is also kind and friendly!
